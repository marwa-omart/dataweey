Introduction¶

This dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. A number of characteristics about the patient are included in each row.

First, I will investigate the size of the problem. Second,I will investigate each characteristic and its coorrelation with not showing up to an appointment in a form of a research question about each characteristic and its impact on the problem of not showing. Third,I'll investigate the impact of the waiting duration and the clinic crowded days on the patient not showing.

Questions¶

  1. How big is the problem?, in other words, How many patients didn't show up to their appointment?
  2. Do SMS reminders decrease the number of absences?
  3. How is age affecting the patient's absence? (Age groups classification)
  4. Are chronic illnesses like hypertension and diabetes affecting patient's absences?
  5. Is alcoholism a prominent factor in patient's absences? Are the absences frequent?
  6. Do disabilities affect patient's absences
  7. Are no-show appointments associated with a certain gender?
  8. Do patients who don't have scholarships affect their absences
  9. Is the period between scheduled day and appointment day affecting the number of no-show appointments?
  10. Do patients tend to skip their appointment if the clinic is crowded?

Limitations:¶

The age 0 was not clear if it is for infants or errant data.
There were 8531 duplciated records by patientid and appointment day and 1855 records out of which didn't attend their appointment.
The medical specialty which each patient is scheduled to is not stated which is important to calculate the crowdness at each medical speciality clinic because if we have for instance 10 patients scheduled to different medical specialities which in return to different physians and therfore different waiting time .
The number of hospitals\clinics available in each neighbourhood is not stated.
Calculating the busiest day or month is irrelevant due to the absence of the medical speciality each patient is scheduled to and the number of clinics\hospitals in each neighbourhood.
Some neighbourhoods are with few medical records.
